Sexual harrassment

Behaviour characterized by the making of unwelcome
inappropriate sexual remarks,
or physical advances
in a workplace or social situation.

Physical Abuse

Physical abuse is any act of hurting or injuring a person.
is an act where one person uses their body in order to inflict intentional harm or injury upon another person.

Emotional Abuse

Emotional abuse involves attempts to frighten, control,
or isolate you.
It’s characterized by a person’s words, actions, and the
consistency of these behaviors.
